After a few years as a tech writer and podcast host, I kept bumping into the same gap: I was talking to tech writers in Romania, India, the US — but couldn't find my own people in Brazil. So three of us ran an experiment. We made a Google Slide deck, a logo built on pre-AI design skills, and dropped a calendar invite into the void. We used our personal Google Meet accounts, because really — who was going to show up? The answer was: more people than the platform could hold. That first meetup became Tech Writing BR, still the only technical writing community in Brazil, now with volunteers across the country, book clubs, online forums, and ongoing webinars.
