In 2020, tech writing was just starting to surface as a career in Brazil, and I had a question I couldn't answer alone: if what we're doing is called tech writing, how do others actually do it? What can we learn from them to scale what we build? So a colleague and I started a podcast. It quickly expanded beyond tech writers to anyone living at the intersection of writing and technology. The Manu&lt;script&gt; became a way to reach professionals across contexts and companies I'd never otherwise access — and to create something of value in exchange for their time. Every episode forced me to deeply research a person's career, industry, and role before we spoke, to understand what they specifically could teach me that no one else could. Interviewing, it turns out, is its own discipline.
