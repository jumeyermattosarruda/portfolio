I've had the luck of doing PMM in two completely different contexts. At VTEX — a Brazilian-born e-commerce platform I joined at 300 employees and left at 1,300 — I worked campaign-driven GTM, with KPIs tied to the sales team: lead acquisition, new contracts, brand awareness. At iConnections, a US company making the shift to product-first, I moved to platform adoption, user research, and engagement, with KPIs tied to the product team. One outbound, one inbound. One expansion-focused, one retention-focused. That range is the point: I learned how to read a business's growth stage and adapt the strategy, rather than repeat a playbook.
